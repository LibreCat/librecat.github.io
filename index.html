Catmandu

- the data processing toolkit -

Access your digital library and research services from the command line with Catmandu.
Download data via OAI-PMH and SRU.
Convert MARC, MODS, Dublin Core an many more.
Generate RDF and speak the Semantic Web.
Index your data into databases such as Solr, Elasticsearch and MongoDB.
Catmandu tools abstracts digital library and research services as data warehouse processes.
